About

A bit more context

I’m a computer science student who likes turning messy ideas into useful, well-structured projects. I enjoy working at the point where technical thinking, product judgment, and business awareness overlap, because that is usually where a project becomes something people actually want to use.

I’m especially interested in building with AI and learning how it can make products smarter, faster, and more helpful without making them harder to use. I like exploring new tools, experimenting with ideas, and figuring out which approaches are practical enough to ship.

Outside of class and project work, I like watching sports and staying active. That balance matters to me, and it helps me bring fresh energy and focus into the work I do.
